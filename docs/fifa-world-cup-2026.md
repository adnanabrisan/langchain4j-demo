## 🧮 New Format (48 Teams)

- 12 groups of 4 teams.
- Top 2 from each group + 8 best third-placed teams advance → **32 teams** to the knockout stage.
- New **Round of 32** knockout phase added.
- Each finalist will play **8 matches** (vs. 7 in previous editions).

## 🔥 Records & Firsts (confirmed before kickoff)

- 🥇 **First** World Cup hosted by **three nations**.
- 🥇 **First** World Cup with **48 teams**.
- 🥇 **First** World Cup with **104 matches**.
- 🏟️ **Estadio Azteca** becomes the **first stadium to host matches in three different World Cups** (1970, 1986, 2026).
- 🌎 Largest geographical footprint of any World Cup to date.
